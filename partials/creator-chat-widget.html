Daily game rules

Goal: find all matching pairs on the board. Each turn you flip two tiles; if they do not match, they flip back after a moment.

After you tap Start, you get a short peek at every tile, then they hide and the countdown runs. Finish before time hits zero.

Each non-matching pair counts as one wrong guess. You only have a limited number before the round ends — shown as flames next to the timer.

About a 25% chance to win loot when you complete the round in time. Wins now roll collection cards only into your Collection. Trade duplicates on the Exchange with trade tokens. One prize-capable play per customer per calendar day unless admin replay mode applies.

Pair attempts, wrong-guess limit, and time depend on today's puzzle; values are enforced server-side.
