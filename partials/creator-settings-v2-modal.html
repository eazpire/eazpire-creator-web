Remove optional app data (keep account)

You can remove profile and app data we store outside Shopify without closing your customer account. Your login and Shopify order history stay with Shopify as required.

  • Removes: profile fields saved in our app database, uploaded photos, design limits and in-app notifications, and cancels a pending scheduled account deletion if any.
  • Does not remove: your Shopify customer login, or order records kept for legal retention. Your saved designs, wardrobe, favorites, mockups, and size profile data are not removed by this action — those are removed only when you fully delete your account after the grace period.

We will email you a confirmation link. Optional data is removed only after you open that link. The link expires in 72 hours.

Delete account & data

You can request deletion of your customer account and associated personal data. We process requests within a reasonable time and as required by law. Some information may be kept where the law requires (for example tax or fraud prevention).

  • Send an email request using the button below (includes your account email), or write to the shop contact address from the email you used to register.
  • We verify ownership of the account before deletion.
  • After the grace period: private and user-uploaded designs and products are removed from our systems (including public listings created from uploads). Public generated designs and products may stay in the shop and be reassigned to the Eazpire creator account. You may lose access to orders and app data in the usual way.
  • Billing balances, gift cards, and creator-linked data are not automatically removed by the standard shop customer deletion flow; they may be retained or handled under internal policy. Order records may remain archived where the law requires.

We will schedule your account for permanent deletion in 7 days and send a confirmation email. You can cancel from that email until then. Orders may remain archived for legal retention where required.
